Edwin Guevara

UX Designer

Edwin Guevara is a UX Designer based out of Seattle, WA. For the past year, he has worked with Reef Cannabis to redesign their e-commerce site. As the sole UX designer, he managed the project from research to production. With a team of developers and key stakeholders throughout the process to troubleshoot along the way. Before making a career change into UX, Edwin worked in the nonprofit sector for nearly a decade. He worked in various roles, most recently as IT Project Manager at the Seattle Art Museum. In this role, he worked with developers and designers on the museum's website and exhibits. While the position was cut short due to the pandemic, he found the experience life-changing.

He promptly enrolled in the User Experience Certificate program at the School of Visual Concepts and the Web Design Associates program at Seattle Central College. As a project manager, he saw the value of learning code and design, which inspired him to return to school. But it was his career in the nonprofits that taught him the power of empathy and clear communication. Edwin is an excellent asset to any team because of his love for collaboration and his background in project management. His experience as a PM means that he can communicate with developers and designers and think about the bigger picture. At the same time, his background in nonprofits makes him empathetic and understanding of the needs of users. He is eager to continue to learn and grow, working along with great designers.
